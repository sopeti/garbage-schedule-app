ゴミ出しカレンダー — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

ゴミ出しカレンダー ("the App") is a garbage collection schedule app for residents of Edogawa-ku, Tokyo. This page explains what information the App handles and how.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The App does not require an account, does not transmit any data to a server, and does not use analytics or tracking.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using iOS secure storage:

  • Selected area — the municipality and neighbourhood you choose (e.g. "江戸川区 / 一之江"). Used solely to look up your collection schedule.
  • Language preference — Japanese or English.
  • Notification settings — whether reminders are enabled and which times you prefer.

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device to alert you before garbage collection days. These notifications are generated entirely on-device; no data is sent to any server to deliver them.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party advertising, analytics, or data-sharing services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
